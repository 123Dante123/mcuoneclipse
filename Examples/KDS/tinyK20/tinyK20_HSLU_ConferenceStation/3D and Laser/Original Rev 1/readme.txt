This folder contains 3D (.123dx, Autodesk 123D Design) and lasercut (.svg) files

Material:
- 4 A4 sheets of 4mm plywood (Birch)

USB Hub:
- 4 M3x16 mm
- 4 M3 knurled insert nuts
- USB cables
- 3D printed enclosure

Camera:
- 1 aluminium rod 100 cm 8mm diameter, 3 pieces: 1.5 cm, 20 cm, 27 cm
- 4 screws M2.5Sx8
- autofocus USB camera module, e.g. ELP OV5640 (5MP)
- 

MuteBox:
- 2 LEDs with current limit resistors
- 2 push buttons
- microcontroller (e.g. tinyK22 board)
- wiring cables
- USB cable
- 4mm plywood for lasercut enclosure
