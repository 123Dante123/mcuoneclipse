This is a demo program for the FRDM-K64F board with Eclipse (Kinetis Design Studio V3.0.0).


Floppy:
- white: Direction
- grey: step

                          2  (SDA)

                          6  PTD3 2 grey step
                          7  PTD2 3 white dir
                          8  PTD0 3 grey step
                          9  PTC4
                          10 PTA0
