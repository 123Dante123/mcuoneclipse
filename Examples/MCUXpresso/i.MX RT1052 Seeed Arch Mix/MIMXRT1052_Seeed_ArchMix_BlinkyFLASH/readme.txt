Project for a 'blinky' on the Seeed Arch Mix (i.MX RT1052) board, running from FLASH.

Notes:
- works out of the box with a NXP LPC-Link.
- for Segger J-Link (Mini EDU), see https://mcuoneclipse.com/2019/10/27/using-segger-j-link-with-qspi-flash-on-nxp-i-mx-boards/
